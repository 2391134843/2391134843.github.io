General

I am currently a third-year master's student at Yunnan University, under the guidance of Prof. Xuan Zhang. I expect to receive my Master's degree certificate in June 2024. I plan to pursue my Ph.D. at PKU under Prof. Zhi Jin. My research interests primarily revolve around addressing various issues related to knowledge graphs using deep learning. This includes knowledge graph completion, multi-modal knowledge graphs, and various downstream applications of knowledge graphs. In my personal life, I enjoy fitness, soccer, and other sports. I hope to have the opportunity to become a university professor in the future. You can learn more about me through platforms like [Researchgate] and [ORCID].
